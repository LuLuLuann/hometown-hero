Visit Bishkek

Enjoy hiking, eating delicious food and shopping.

Top three activities to do in and around Bishkek.

Watch an opera or a ballet at the opera house.

The Kyrgyz National Opera and Ballet Theater, built in the 1920s, is a great place to watch an opera or ballet full of talent and passion. Listen to an opera here and you feel it in your soul.

Go hiking in Ala Archa Park.

Ala-Archa Nature Park is a 16,485 hectare alpine national park in the Tian Shan mountains, 35 km south of Bishkek. A popular and affordable local hiking group is the Trekking Union of Kyrgyzstan (TUK).

Watch a game of Kok Boru!

The national sport of Kok Boru, (also called "Ulak Tartysh"), is very exciting. Watch teams of men on horses compete in this game of strength, agility and horsemanship with a 75 pound animal carcass.
